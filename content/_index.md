Hello! I'm Sairam, a graduate student at UC San Diego advised by [Loris D'Antoni](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~ldantoni/).

I'm interested in the reliability of language models and formal methods. My current work uses grammars as a lens on correctness: both for [constraining language models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.05754) during decoding and for generating [structured inputs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05887) that test compilers well.
