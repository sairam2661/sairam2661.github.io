*Making compilers more reliable.*

## About Me.

Hi, I'm Sairam, a graduate student at UC San Diego working with Loris D'Antoni's research group.

I'm interested in Programming Languages, Formal Methods, and the reliability of language models. My current work uses grammars as a lens on correctness — both for constraining language models during decoding and for generating structured inputs that stress-test compilers. In both cases, the grammar is what turns a probabilistic or exploratory process into something you can reason about.
