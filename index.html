About

I am a second-year graduate student at UCSD working with Loris D'Antoni. My research focuses on constrained generation for language models and their applications to compiler testing and program synthesis. Previously, I worked on low-latency trading systems at J.P. Morgan and Morgan Stanley. I received my BE in Computer Science from PSG Tech in 2023.
