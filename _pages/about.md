Hello! I’m a first-year Master’s student in Computer Science (MS CS) at [UCSD](https://cse.ucsd.edu), specializing in Programming Languages and AI. I’m currently a part of the [Loris Research Group](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~ldantoni/), where I study Constrained Decoding for Fuzzing, exploring techniques to improve program synthesis and security testing with structured generative models.

Karpagam](https://scholar.google.co.in/citations?user=8x3AVVwAAAAJ&hl=en), earning myself a Project of the Year award.

I’m actively seeking Summer 2025 internship opportunities where I can apply my expertise in software engineering, AI research, and program analysis to tackle challenging problems.
