Hello! I'm a first-year Master’s student in Computer Science (MS CS) at [UCSD](https://cse.ucsd.edu), with a strong interest in the real-world applications of Programming Languages and AI concepts. Recently, I have been focused on SMT solvers, Responsible AI, and Program Synthesis.

Before my MS, I graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from PSG Tech in May 2023, where I worked on [Explainable AI](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aQ6Hq9qHFgN3TMpncKUIBMIxRElsm5ZY/view?usp=sharing) under [Dr. G.R. Karpagam](https://scholar.google.co.in/citations?user=8x3AVVwAAAAJ&hl=en). After graduation, I joined Morgan Stanley as a software engineer on the Credit Valuations and Adjustments team, where I worked on an intersection of distributed computing, finance and software engineering.

Outside of academics and work, I enjoy competitive Pokémon (feel free to send me a challenge on [Showdown](https://play.pokemonshowdown.com)!), playing the piano, and cooking.
