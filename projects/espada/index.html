Espada Five

Building the Perfect 5-a-side Football Team using Data Science

🏆 Winner of Advolution 3.0!

Ever wondered what would happen if we let data science pick a 5-a-side football team? That’s exactly what we did with Espada Five! Traditional football analytics focus on 11-a-side matches, but 5-a-side is a completely different ball game (pun intended!).

The Game Plan 🎯

We set out to create the ultimate 5-a-side team using a 1-1-2-1 formation through statistical analysis. Here’s how we broke it down:

Step 1: Getting Our Data in Shape

Initial correlation analysis showing relationships between player attributes. Who knew passing accuracy could be so colorful?

Step 2: The Master Plan

Breaking down our 1-1-2-1 formation requirements through normalized statistics. Because every position needs its own special sauce!

Player Selection Process 🎮

The Goal Getter (Forward)

Finding our star striker - because someone needs to put the ball in the net!

The Midfield Maestro

Analyzing midfield candidates - looking for the perfect balance of attack and defense.

Defender analysis - because even Messi needs to get past someone!

The Super Subs

Finding versatile substitutes who can play multiple positions - our Swiss Army knives!

The Last Line of Defense (Goalkeeper)

Finding our perfect shot-stopper - because in 5-a-side, your goalkeeper needs to be part-keeper, part-sweeper, and part-quarterback!

The Dream Team 🌟

The final squad - statistically proven to be awesome! (Now if only they could play together in real life...)

Technical Highlights

  • Comprehensive feature engineering for 5-a-side specific attributes
  • Position-based weighted scoring system

  • Formation-specific optimization
  • Substitute selection based on versatility metrics

Fun Facts 🎉

  • Processed data from thousands of professional players
